PREDULIVE EDUTECH FOUNDATION

Predulive Edutech Foundation’s primary mission is to inspire, aware, educate & employ by driving large-scale job creation through entrepreneurship, innovation and skills development. The lack of appropriate training and no proper formal education in small towns and cities are also some of the reasons for unemployment. So, Predulive provides an opportunity to discover their capabilities in an encouraging environment to grow through various training & workshops in the field such as electronics, telecom, agriculture, aviation, healthcare, tourism, and hospitality.

Building Awareness among People.

The youth, especially, those living in the rural areas are unaware of the new job opportunities. The lack of appropriate training and no proper formal education in small towns and cities are also some of the reasons for unemployment. So that Predulive provide an opportunity to discover their capabilities an encouraging environment to grow through various training & workshops in the field such as electronics, telecom, agriculture, Aviation, BFSI retail, healthcare, tourism & hospitality.

Working towards Rural Development.

Predulive always try to Collaborate with multiple startups of different -2 industries like Drones, Medical & Agriculture etc. for building a good ecosystem for our youth & our unemployed workers.With a lot of employers complaining about the lack of requisite skills in new entrants, Predulive aim is to offer skill training to the youth and make them industry ready.

Spreading Career Awareness

Predulive Edutech Foundation, through the implementation of project based learning and hands-on activities, hopes to bring back the joy in learning. With the use of innovative approaches to teaching, they hope to ignite the spark of curiosity in children and gently chisel them to take form befitting the world standard.

Priviledge children.

Career Counselling is the pivotal cog in a student’s journey. A good career counsellor makes the perfect roadmap for students and based on which they can achieve their goals. Predulive provide a great counsellor which can give the right direction to the child alongside advice the parents to take up progressive decisions.

Right Career Choices. Planning the College Journey.

Great readers become great counsellor and great motivators. A career counsellor is a teacher and a motivator, so building awareness about different career fields and prospects add value to the counselling profession. Predulive Career Counsellors can up their ante with this course and they will have answers to all the possible questions asked by students.
